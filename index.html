Arts & Humanities Research Computing, informally known as DARTH (Digital Arts + Humanities), provides consultation, instruction, project management, and technical development for FAS faculty engaged in digital humanities projects at Harvard University.

Projects

Fionn Folklore Database

A digital catalog of oral histories related to the Gaelic folklore hero, Fionn mac Cumhaill

Mapping Color in History

MCiH uses scientific data from material analyses of pigments to examine Asian painting from a historical perspective

Remapping the Očhéti Šakówiŋ Oyáte

Remapping the Očhéti Šakówiŋ Oyáte aims to record Očhéti Šakówiŋ Oyáte (O.S.) community members’ and tribal citizens’ storytelling.

Tsumeb Mine Notebook

The Tsumeb Mine Notebok documents the rich legacy of diverse and informative mineralogical material of the Tsumeb Mine in Namibia.
